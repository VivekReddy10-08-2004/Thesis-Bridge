# Pre-Investment Research Memo

## Thesis
Analyze the historical impact of prolonged elevated interest rates on the Net Interest Margins (NIM) and deposit beta of US regional banks with under $50B in assets over the last 4 quarters.

## Executive Summary
The analysis of case studies from various US regional banks indicates that prolonged elevated interest rates can lead to fluctuations in Net Interest Margins (NIM) and deposit beta, affecting the banks' profitability and competitiveness.
