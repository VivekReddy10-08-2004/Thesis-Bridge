# Pre-Investment Research Memo

## Thesis
Should a boutique firm adopt direct indexing for taxable U.S. equity portfolios?

## Executive Summary
Direct indexing offers a promising approach for managing taxable U.S. equity portfolios, with potential benefits including tax-loss harvesting, customization, and improved after-tax returns. With the market expected to grow significantly, adopting direct indexing could be a strategic move for a boutique firm.
