# Pre-Investment Research Memo

## Thesis
Historical revenue impact of GLP-1 weight loss drugs on cardiovascular medical device companies.

## Executive Summary
The introduction of GLP-1 weight loss drugs has shown significant potential in reducing cardiovascular risk, but their impact on the medical device industry is still being assessed. Studies indicate a potential decrease in device usage due to the effectiveness of these drugs in managing weight and related health conditions.
