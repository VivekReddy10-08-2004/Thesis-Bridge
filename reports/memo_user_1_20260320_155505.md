## Thesis
Should a boutique wealth firm launch alternative investment model portfolios for accredited clients?

## Executive Summary
The case studies demonstrate a growing interest in alternative investments among wealth management firms and their clients. Firms like Fidelity, Proteus, and iCapital have launched alternative investment model portfolios, which have shown potential for growth and diversification. However, it is essential to carefully evaluate the risks and caveats associated with alternative investments.
