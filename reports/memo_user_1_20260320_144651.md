# Pre-Investment Research Memo

## Thesis
Should a boutique firm implement AI-assisted advisor note summarization to increase advisor capacity?

## Executive Summary
The use of AI-assisted advisor note summarization can lead to significant efficiency gains, cost savings, and potential revenue growth for a boutique wealth/asset management firm. With a baseline annual revenue of $1,000,000, the potential annual impact could range from $40,000 to $285,000, depending on the level of implementation and adoption.
